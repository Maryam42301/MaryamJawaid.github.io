Northwind Analysis in MySQL

This project involved performing data analysis on the Northwind database using MySQL and creating insightful visualizations with Tableau. Some highlights include:
Customer Analysis: Unveiling insights into Retention Rate, Churn Rate, Customer Lifetime Value, and Customer Segmentation based on RFM . Performance Analysis: Evaluating Top 5 products by Revenue, Top 5 Employees, and Order Lead time . Revenue Analysis: Delving into Growth Rate, Monthly Revenue Comparison, and Average Category Revenue . Inventory Analysis: Optimizing inventory management for streamlined processes by analyzing Inventory on hands.
RFM Analysis: Leveraging Recency, Frequency, and Monetary metrics for targeted insights.
